Traditional vs Modern Templates

Choose between classic layouts built for conservative corporate industries or bold multi-column designs crafted for dynamic tech-roles and modern startups.

Traditional: Clean single-column vertical layout with centered typography—highly optimized for formal corporate industries and standard ATS parsers.
Modern: High-impact asymmetric layout featuring a stylish left sidebar, content-driven structural grids, and clean developer icons.
Instant Toggle: Switch between classic structures and creative designs directly from the live preview dashboard without losing any data.

Navigation & Action Controls

Take full control of your resume building process with intuitive navigation steps, dynamic section expansion, and instantaneous multi-format compilations.

Step Navigation: Effortlessly jump between resume form sections using Previous and Next wizards without experiencing any layout lag or data loss.
Dynamic Field Injection: Add multiple education degrees or job experiences instantly with a single click on the smart cloning handler.
One-Click Compilation: Trigger the highly optimized rendering engine via the Generate PDF action control to output a polished, recruitment-ready resume.
